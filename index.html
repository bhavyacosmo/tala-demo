Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Who is this workshop for?

This workshop is best suited for Music teachers, homeroom/class teachers, performing arts teachers, and anyone working with children from ages 2 to 7.

I am not a music teacher. How will this help me?

The sessions introduce you to a play-based approach that can transform your Circle time and help you integrate music with subjects like math, reading, and social studies.

Where is the location of the workshop?

Bangalore: NSK (Nava Spoorti Kendra), 2J49+5H7, Unnamed Road, Sagayapura, Richards Town, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560084. Google Map →

Pune: MIS — Mahindra International School, P-26, MIDC, Hinjewadi Phase 1 Rd, Hinjawadi Rajiv Gandhi Infotech Park, Pune, Maharashtra 411057. Google Map →

What are the accommodation arrangements?

Bangalore (Residential): Participants stay in single or twin-sharing rooms with comfortable beds and solar/geyser water showers — in the same vicinity as the workshop venue.

Pune: Tāla will not be providing a stay. Local hotels are available within 10 minutes walking distance and we can share budget-friendly recommendations.

What will I receive upon completion?

Participants receive a Professional Participation Certificate, comprehensive digital course materials, templates, lesson plans, and post-workshop support. You also gain exclusive access to the Tāla Affiliate Program.

What should I bring for this workshop?

All learning materials, instruments, and stationery are provided. Just bring your personal belongings and an open mind! 🙂

If you are presenting at the event, feel free to bring your unique attire or specific instrument to showcase your culture.
